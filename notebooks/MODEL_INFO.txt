## MODEL INFO ##
- I'm putting this here temporarily to help users understand each model as the number grows.

* BASELINE: trained only on clean data from the original datset.
* 50_50_SPLIT_TAPE: trained on a 50/50 split of tape vandalized images and clean data.
* 70_30_SPLIT_TAPE: trained on a 70/30 split of tape vandalized images and clean data,
  with Albumentations augmentation applied to all images (~2x effective dataset size).
  Augmentations include: horizontal flips, brightness/contrast jitter, hue-saturation shift,
  Gaussian blur, CLAHE, image compression noise, and random shadow.
